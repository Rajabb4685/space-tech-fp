Timeline

Digital Photography

1969

  • “Digital Photography” was discovered by NASA. NASA invented the digital camera in 1969 because they made a picture of space and the camera at the time was very good so Robert Nathan, a scientist at NASA invented the digital camera with a lens, something that all of our phones have. This invention helped NASA take better images and help them get more complex.
  • “Whether you take pictures and videos with a DSLR camera or cell phone, or even capture action on the go with a device like a GoPro Hero, you’re using NASA technology. The CMOS active pixel sensor in most digital image-capturing devices was invented when NASA needed to miniaturize cameras for interplanetary missions. It is also widely used in medical imaging and dental X-ray devices.” - NASA

April 24, 1990

Hubble Space Telescope

  • “Hubble Space Telescope” the United States space agency NASA with contributions from the European Space Agency.
  • The Hubble Space Telescope is named after the American astronomer Edwin Hubble because he made many discoveries of the expanding universe. This invention played a big role in complexity and discovery in the space field. The Hubble Space Telescope made 1.3 million discoveries since 1990. This invention helps us know that the universe is expanding.

This invention is something I would say most of us have used because when you go to the doctor they use this in your ear. October 15, 1997

Cassini huygen probe (satellite)

  • “Cassini Huygens Probe (Satellite)”was invented by NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory & Thales Alenia Space (a french and Italian aerospace manufacturer).
  • This invention was a collaboration between NASA, the European Space Agency, and the Italian Space Agency to send a space probe to study its rings and natural satellites including planet Saturn and its system. It was invented on October 15, 1997. Its mission was to study and take pictures of Saturn for about 20 years. Check out this website by “NASA” it shows the parts of the Cassini Huygens probe. Its visuals are outstanding.

You can look at even international space stations but search in the app. This was beautifully filled with beautiful space images and nice designs that are easy to use.
